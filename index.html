Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-02

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game Donut Dash (package name: com.bear.donutdash) published by Bear Pixel Studio.

1) Summary

  • We do not require account creation and we do not provide cloud save.
  • Game progress is stored locally on your device.
  • We use Google services (Firebase Analytics, Google Play Billing, and ads if enabled) which may process limited data.

2) Data we collect

We may collect limited data to operate, measure, and improve the game:

  • App interactions / usage data (e.g., app opens, screens viewed, gameplay events) for analytics and performance improvements.
  • Device & app identifiers such as an app-instance / installation identifier (used by analytics). If ads are enabled, we (or our ad partners) may also use advertising identifiers such as the Android Advertising ID and/or App Set ID depending on device settings and SDK configuration.
  • Diagnostics (e.g., performance information, crash-related data) if diagnostic/crash reporting is enabled.
  • In-app purchase information (e.g., product ID and purchase status) to deliver purchased items and restore purchases. Payment details are handled by Google Play and are not received by us.

3) Local game data (no account / no cloud save)

  • Progress is stored locally on your device (for example: your level progress and settings).
  • If you uninstall the game, local data stored on the device is typically removed with the app. We do not offer account-based syncing or cloud backup within the game.

4) How we use data

  • To measure retention/session length, understand gameplay behavior, and improve performance and user experience.
  • To show ads (if enabled) and help prevent fraud/abuse related to ads.
  • To process and validate in-app purchases and deliver purchased content (if purchases are available).

5) Third-party services

We use third-party services that may collect and process data according to their own policies:

  • Google Firebase / Google Analytics (analytics & measurement)
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases)
  • Ad providers (for example, Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK) if ads are enabled

6) Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed and/or shared with the service providers listed above only to provide analytics, ads, and purchase functionality.

7) Security

We do not operate our own user data servers. We rely on reputable third-party providers (such as Google) and use reasonable measures to protect data. For example, data transmission is handled using secure connections where applicable, and access to tools (Firebase/Ad services) is limited to authorized use for operating the game.

8) Data retention and deletion

  • We retain data only as long as necessary to operate and improve the game, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Retention periods may vary depending on the provider settings.
  • You can reset or limit the Android Advertising ID in your device settings (this may affect personalized ads).
  • If you contact us, we can provide guidance on deletion options available through our providers (for example, deleting a Firebase installation identifier).

9) Your choices

  • You can limit ad personalization and reset your Advertising ID via Android/Google settings.
  • You can contact us for privacy/data questions.

10) Children’s privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
